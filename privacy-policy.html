Lock In Twin

Privacy Policy

What we collect, what we don't, and why every permission exists.

Last updated: March 2026
TL;DR

Lock In Twin does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Ever. Your settings stay on your device. Nothing leaves your browser.

01 —

Overview

Lock In Twin ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that detects when you go idle and displays a focus reminder alert. It was built to help you stay on task — not to track you.

This policy covers the Extension only. It does not apply to any third-party services or websites you visit while using Chrome.

02 —

Data We Collect

The Extension stores the following settings locally on your device using Chrome's built-in sync storage:

alertType

Which alert style you selected: game, overlay, or banner.

LOCAL ONLY
idleMinutes

How many minutes of idle time before the alert fires.

LOCAL ONLY
enabled

Whether the extension is currently turned on or off.

LOCAL ONLY

That's it. No account. No profile. No analytics. No usage data.

03 —

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or URLs you visit
  • Page content or text on any website
  • Keystrokes, clicks, or mouse movements
  • Personally identifiable information of any kind
  • IP address or location data
  • Device information or hardware identifiers
  • Screenshots or visual data from your screen
  • Idle duration logs or timestamps
04 —

Permissions & Why They're Needed

Every permission the Extension requests has a specific, narrow purpose. Here's exactly what each one does:

idle

Detects when your browser goes idle (no mouse, keyboard, or screen activity). This is the core feature — without it, nothing triggers.

READ ONLY
storage

Saves your settings (alert type, idle timer duration, on/off state) locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

LOCAL ONLY
tabs

Used to identify which tab is currently active when the idle timer fires, so the alert overlay appears in the right place. Tab URLs and content are never read.

READ ONLY
scripting

Allows the background service worker to communicate with the content script running on the active tab. Used only to trigger the alert display.

LOCAL ONLY
activeTab

Grants temporary access to the tab you're currently on when the idle alert triggers. No persistent or background tab access.

TEMPORARY
host_permissions: http://* & https://*

Required so the content script that displays the alert overlay can be pre-loaded on any page. Since idle time can expire on any website, the script must be available everywhere. The script only listens for a trigger signal — it does not read, modify, or transmit any page content.

ALL SITES
05 —

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. No data is sent anywhere outside your browser.

The alert sound is generated entirely using the browser's built-in Web Audio API — no audio files are loaded from external sources.

06 —

Children's Privacy

Lock In Twin does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The Extension is fully compliant with COPPA as no data collection occurs.

07 —

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the updated version will be published at this same URL and the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of the Extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
